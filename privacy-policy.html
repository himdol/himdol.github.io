3. 만 14세 미만 아동의 개인정보

만 3세 이상 가입 가능하나, 만 14세 미만은 법정대리인(보호자) 동의가 필요합니다. 동의 없이 수집된 경우 즉시 파기하며, 법정대리인은 언제든 자녀 정보의 열람·정정·삭제·처리정지를 요청할 수 있습니다.

TheCalorieLog Privacy Policy

Effective: May 20, 2026 · Last updated: May 20, 2026
Service: TheCalorieLog · Contact: himdol8833@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

  • Account: email, nickname, social login identifier (Google/Apple)
  • Profile: gender, birth date, height, activity level
  • Health records: food logs (name, calories, nutrients, memo), weight, steps, calories burned
  • Images: food photos for AI analysis (not stored on the server)
  • Device: timezone, OS / app version, language
  • Automatic: access logs, usage records, error logs

2. How We Use Information

  • Account management and identity verification
  • AI analysis of food photos to estimate calories and nutrients
  • Tracking and displaying intake, expenditure, steps, and weight trends
  • Predicted/estimated weight, missions, streaks (XP)
  • Service improvement, error monitoring, security response
  • Legal compliance and dispute resolution

3. Children's Information

For users under 14, we require verifiable consent from a parent or legal guardian. Data collected without such consent is deleted immediately. Guardians may request access, correction, deletion, or processing suspension at any time.

4. Third Parties and Processors

  • Google LLC — Google Identity (social login), Gemini API (food image analysis; images are discarded after analysis and not stored on our servers).
  • Apple Inc. — Sign in with Apple on iOS.

We do not sell or share personal information with other third parties without consent.

5. Retention and Deletion

  • Retained while the account is active.
  • Permanent deletion immediately upon account withdrawal — irreversible.
  • Dormant mode disables the account while preserving records; restored on re-login.
  • Where required by law, retained for the statutory period.
  • Food photos are discarded after analysis and not stored.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, delete, restrict processing, or withdraw consent at any time — via in-app controls or by contacting us. We will respond without undue delay after verifying identity.

7. Security

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit
  • Auth tokens stored in secure device storage (Keychain / Keystore / Secure Store)
  • No password storage (social login only)
  • Least-privilege access and access logging
  • Periodic security reviews

8. Cookies

The app does not use web cookies. Automatic data collection is limited to the items listed in Section 1.

9. Contact

Email: himdol8833@gmail.com

10. Changes

Changes are announced at least 7 days (30 days for material changes) in advance via in-app notice.
